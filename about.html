About the Course

This intensive 3-day ESG Masterclass helps boards and executives embed ESG into strategy, governance, risk management, investment decisions, reporting and long-term organisational resilience.

Course rationale

Leading Sustainable Governance

This course is designed specifically for Directors, Board Members, CEOs, Executives, Risk Professionals, Sustainability Leaders, Audit & Compliance Committees and Senior Decision-Makers operating within the South African and broader African business environment.

GRC

Governance Frameworks

King IV™, Integrated Reporting, JSE Sustainability Disclosure Guidance and corporate governance best practice.

ESG

ESG Standards

IFRS Sustainability Disclosure Standards, IFRS S1 & S2, ESG reporting, climate governance and SDG alignment.

AFR

African Context

Practical application for public sector, financial services, infrastructure, mining, energy, manufacturing and development realities.

What are the goals?

By the end of the course, participants will be able to:

Understand ESG principles and strategic business implications

Interpret global and African ESG frameworks and sustainability standards

Strengthen Board oversight and governance of ESG matters

Integrate ESG into enterprise strategy and risk management

Understand climate risk, sustainability reporting and stakeholder expectations

Improve ESG governance and accountability structures

Evaluate ESG investment and sustainable finance considerations

Build practical ESG implementation roadmaps

Enhance organisational sustainability and resilience

Improve ESG reporting readiness and transparency

Align sustainability initiatives with SDGs and Net Zero ambitions
