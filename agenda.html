The Course Content

Programme Agenda

A practical 3-day ESG leadership, governance and sustainability curriculum covering 14 modules from ESG foundations to boardroom simulations and ESG action planning.

3Intensive Training Days
14Core Learning Modules
1ESG Action Plan

Day 1 introduces ESG, sustainable leadership, business sustainability, board oversight, climate governance and social value creation.

Day One

Module 1: Introduction to ESG & Sustainable Leadership

  • Understanding ESG fundamentals
  • ESG vs Sustainability vs CSR
  • Evolution of ESG globally and in Africa
  • Why ESG matters to Boards and Executives
  • ESG as a driver of long-term value creation
  • Stakeholder capitalism and responsible leadership
  • Emerging ESG trends in Africa
Day One

Module 2: Business Sustainability & Future-Ready Organisations

  • Sustainability as a strategic imperative
  • Business resilience and long-term sustainability
  • ESG and competitive advantage
  • ESG integration into corporate strategy
  • ESG innovation and transformation
  • Sustainability and enterprise value
Day One

Module 3: The Role of Boards & Executives in ESG Governance

  • Fiduciary duties and ESG accountability
  • Board oversight responsibilities
  • ESG governance structures and committees
  • Executive accountability and ESG KPIs
  • Embedding ESG into governance frameworks
  • Ethical leadership and organisational culture
  • Board ESG maturity models
Day One

Module 4: Environmental (“E”) Factors & Climate Governance

  • Climate change and business risk
  • Carbon emissions and decarbonisation
  • Water stewardship and biodiversity
  • Energy transition and renewable energy
  • Waste management and circular economy
  • Environmental compliance obligations
  • Physical and transition climate risks
Day One

Module 5: Social (“S”) Factors & Stakeholder Value Creation

  • Human capital and workforce wellbeing
  • Diversity, equity and inclusion
  • Labour relations and employee engagement
  • Health, safety and wellness governance
  • Community engagement and social licence to operate
  • Human rights and ethical supply chains
  • Transformation and socio-economic development

Day 2 focuses on governance, ethical leadership, ESG frameworks, risk management, reporting, assurance and sustainable finance.

Day Two

Module 6: Governance (“G”) & Ethical Corporate Leadership

  • Corporate governance and ethics
  • Anti-corruption and anti-bribery frameworks
  • Board independence and effectiveness
  • Executive remuneration and ESG metrics
  • Whistleblowing and ethics programmes
  • Transparency and accountability
  • Data governance and cybersecurity
Day Two

Module 7: ESG Frameworks, Standards & Regulatory Environment

  • King IV™ guidance
  • JSE Sustainability Disclosure Guidance
  • ESG disclosure expectations
  • Climate governance obligations
  • Emerging African ESG regulatory trends
Day Two

Module 8: ESG Risk Management & Enterprise Strategy

  • ESG risk identification and assessment
  • ESG integration into Enterprise Risk Management (ERM)
  • Climate scenario planning
  • Materiality assessments
  • ESG performance measurement and KPIs
  • ESG strategy alignment
  • Building resilient organisations
Day Two

Module 9: ESG Reporting, Disclosure & Assurance

  • Sustainability reporting principles
  • ESG data management and reporting systems
  • Materiality and stakeholder disclosures
  • ESG assurance and verification
  • Avoiding greenwashing
  • Investor-focused ESG reporting
  • Integrated reporting best practices
Day Two

Module 10: ESG Investing & Sustainable Finance

  • ESG investing trends globally and in Africa
  • Sustainable finance principles
  • ESG ratings and investor expectations
  • Responsible investment strategies
  • Impact investing and blended finance
  • Green financing opportunities
  • Accessing climate finance

Day 3 moves into ESG strategy, implementation, transformation, boardroom simulations and practical action planning.

Day Three

Module 11: ESG Strategy Development & Implementation

  • Developing ESG implementation frameworks
  • ESG transformation roadmaps
  • Integrating ESG into organisational strategy
  • ESG operating models
  • Leadership alignment and culture change
  • ESG governance structures
  • ESG technology and digital enablement
Day Three

Module 12: ESG as a Business Transformation Driver

  • ESG and organisational competitiveness
  • ESG and reputation management
  • ESG and customer trust
  • ESG and talent attraction
  • ESG-driven innovation
  • Sustainable supply chains
  • ESG opportunities in African markets
Day Three

Module 13: Boardroom ESG Simulations & Executive Decision-Making

  • Climate crisis response
  • Governance and ethics scandals
  • Social unrest and stakeholder pressure
  • ESG reporting controversies
  • Investor activism and Board accountability
  • Sustainability-related reputational risks
  • Board decision-making under ESG pressure
  • Crisis communication and stakeholder engagement
Day Three

Module 14: Building an ESG Action Plan

  • ESG action plan
  • Governance improvement framework
  • ESG reporting roadmap
  • Sustainability strategy
  • Stakeholder engagement framework
