Booking terms

Availability, payment and cancellations

All conference, accommodation, airport shuttle and activity bookings are subject to availability. Accommodation, shuttle and activity/excursion arrangements require upfront payment before reservations can be confirmed. Group discount packages are confirmed once booking details are verified. Last-minute cancellations or changes may incur supplier penalties.

Group bookings

Bring your support team together.

Team discounts are available for 3 or more delegates booked by the same organisation, with custom packages for 10+ delegates.
