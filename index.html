Why choose this training course?

Board-level ESG training contextualised for African business realities.

The programme is adapted from leading international ESG director programmes and contextualised for African regulatory, governance, sustainability, infrastructure, social impact, mining, energy, public sector, financial services, manufacturing and development realities. It integrates King IV™, JSE Sustainability Disclosure Guidance, IFRS Sustainability Standards, climate governance, stakeholder capitalism and African ESG risk considerations.

01

King IV™ & Governance

Strengthen board oversight, accountability, ethics and governance structures for ESG matters.

02

IFRS S1 & S2

Understand sustainability disclosure standards and the board’s role in reporting readiness.

03

Risk & Resilience

Integrate ESG into enterprise risk management, climate risk and long-term strategy.

04

Sustainable Finance

Evaluate ESG investing, impact investing, green financing and investor expectations.
